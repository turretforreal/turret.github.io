ENGINE OIL FILTER

The engine oil filter should be replaced with a new filter at every engine oil change.

ENGINE OIL FILTER SELECTION

This manufacturer's engines have a full-flow type oil filter. Use a filter of this type for replacement. The quality of replacement filters varies considerably.
